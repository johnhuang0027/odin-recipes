Mapo Tofu

Spicy Tofu Dish

This Chinese dish is a very popular dish worldwide and has many variations. This dish is known for its spice and its massive flavor. Usually enjoyed with rice, this dish makes for a fulfilling meal.

Ingredients

  • Soft Tofu
  • Ground Beef/Pork
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sichuan Peppercorn
  • Cornstarch
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Fermented Chili Bean Paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Xiaoxing wine
  • Scallions

Steps to Cook

  1. Cut tofu into cubes and boil for a minute
  2. Render ground meat and add garlic, ginger, and peppercorns in oil
  3. Mix mapo tofu sauce in a separate bowl: fermented chili bean paste, soy sauce, and xiaoxing wine
  4. Strain tofu and add to meat and spice mixture
  5. Add sauce mixture and mix
  6. Mix in cornstarch slurry using water and cornstarch to thicken
  7. Serve up and optionally garnish with scallions
