Chicken Katsu

Crispy Chicken over rice

Chicken Katsu is a Japanese style fried chicken recipe. Usually served with japanese curry and rice, the chicken stays crispy and has tons of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast filet
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps to Cook

  1. Flatten your filet of chicken breast
  2. Cover the Chicken Breast in flour
  3. Then dunk the floured breast in beaten egg
  4. Cover the chicken with panko bread crumbs
  5. Fry your chicken breast with oil
