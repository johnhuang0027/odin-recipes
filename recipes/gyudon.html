Gyudon

This Japanese rice and beef dish is extremely simple yet very satiating and satisfying. This dish can be customized to your tastes with different addons.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Sliced Onions
  • Dashi Stock
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Sake
  • Rice

Steps to Cook

  1. Off the heat mix the sauce ingredients in a pan using: Dashi stock, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and sake
  2. Add a layer of onions in the sauce
  3. Add layer of beef on top of the onions
  4. Turn on the heat and cover until boiling
  5. When boiling take off lid and continue cooking for 3-4 minutes
  6. Serve ontop of rice and enjoy
